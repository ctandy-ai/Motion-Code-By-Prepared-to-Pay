### 7. **Typography Refinement**
**Current**: Inter + Rajdhani + Orbitron
**Proposed**: Simplified professional hierarchy

**Font Stack**:
- **Headings**: `Rajdhani` (Bold, 600-800 weight) - Keep for personality
- **Body/UI**: `Inter` (400-600 weight) - Clean, readable
- **Stats/Numbers**: `JetBrains Mono` (Monospace) - Better number alignment than Orbitron
- **Remove**: Orbitron (too gaming-focused for enterprise)

**Reasoning**: JetBrains Mono is monospaced (numbers align perfectly in tables/stats) but more professional than Orbitron's sci-fi aesthetic
