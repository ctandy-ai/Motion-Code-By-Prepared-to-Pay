What athletes get (mobile-first, thumb-friendly):

* **Today tab**: readiness slider (RPE), quick flags (“sore calves”), **Today’s session** with 1-tap **Details**, **Start Session**, and built-in **rest timers**.
* **Exercise drawer**: video placeholder, concise **coaching notes**, **progressions/constraints** pattern ready to wire to your library.
* **Program tab**: this week view + upcoming tiles, driven by the templates you build in the coach console (White/Blue/Black).
* **Progress tab**: **belt readiness bar** + last **test gateways** (Triple Hop symmetry, ADR, Flying 20).
* **Messages tab**: lightweight chat with coach (threads later if you want).
* **Profile tab**: basics + notification toggles.
* Bottom nav: **Today · Program · Progress · Messages · Profile**.

Design choices aimed at retention + compliance:

* One-hand use, large touch targets, **floating + quick-log**.
* Ultra-clear microcopy (“fly-ins”, “shin vertical”), no fluff.
* Minimal decisions per screen; session ≠ wall of text.

### How this ships with your Replit backend

1. **Name & bundle**

   * Client app name: **StrideCode Go** (Coach console = **StrideCode Pro**).
   * Drop this file into your Replit project under `/static/client.html` (or keep separate as `go.stridecode.app` later).

2. **Auth model**

   * Email + magic link (Clerk/Auth0) or OTP via SMS; map to `athlete_id`, `belt`, `program_id`.

3. **Endpoints to add**

   * `GET /api/athlete/me` → belt, goals, readiness baseline.
   * `GET /api/program/week?athlete_id=` → seven-day plan (exercises with sets/reps/notes).
   * `POST /api/logs/readiness` → { rpe, flags[] }.
   * `POST /api/logs/set` → { exercise_id, set_no, load, reps, rpe }.
   * `GET/POST /api/messages` → simple thread with coach.
   * `GET /api/tests/latest` → gateways (TH Sym %, ADR, Flying 20).

4. **Wire-ups (quick)**

   * Replace mocked arrays with `fetch()` to those endpoints.
   * Exercise **drawer** loads `notes/progressions/constraints` from your MBS library.
   * Timer + “Mark Set Done” posts to `/logs/set`.

5. **Brand & polish**

   * Switch brand palette to your Stride/P2P colours.
   * Drop in real exercise thumbnails or 3-sec muted loops.
