1. Template taxonomy v1 (what you build now)

Naming convention (don’t skip this):

Pathway_Phase_Level_Environment_Frequency

Examples:

ACL_P2_Blue_Gym_3x

Hams_P3_Blue_Field+Gym_3x

Perf_P4_Black_InSeason_2x

A. ACL pathway

Phases:

P1 = Early load tolerance / basics

P2 = Strength + capacity

P3 = Power + COD / change of direction

P4 = Return-to-sport / in-season

Levels:

White = newbie / low training age

Blue = intermediate

Black = advanced / high training age

Environments:

Gym

Field+Gym

Home

Build these first (9 templates):

ACL_P1_White_Home_3x – early stage, minimal gear

ACL_P1_Blue_Gym_3x – machine + basic barbell

ACL_P2_Blue_Gym_3x – main workhorse, most ACLs will live here

ACL_P2_Black_Gym_4x – athletes with higher training age

ACL_P2_Blue_Field+Gym_3x – starting to integrate running/COD

ACL_P3_Blue_Field+Gym_3x – higher-speed work, decel, COD

ACL_P3_Black_Field+Gym_4x – performance return higher level

ACL_P4_Blue_Field+Gym_2x – in-season ACL maintenance

ACL_P4_Black_Field+Gym_2x – high-level in-season ACL athlete

B. Hamstring pathway

Same logic.

Build these (6 templates):

Hams_P1_White_Home_3x – acute / minimal gear

Hams_P1_Blue_Gym_3x – intro strength

Hams_P2_Blue_Gym_3x – Nordics / RDLs / hinges

Hams_P2_Black_Gym_4x – higher density

Hams_P3_Blue_Field+Gym_3x – sprint integration

Hams_P4_Blue_Field+Gym_2x – in-season hamstring maintenance

C. Performance-only pathway

For no major injury / S&C focus.

Build these (6 templates):

Perf_P2_White_Gym_2x – gen pop / low training age

Perf_P2_Blue_Gym_3x – bread-and-butter perf template

Perf_P3_Blue_Field+Gym_3x – field sport athletes off-season

Perf_P3_Black_Field+Gym_4x – high performance / off-season

Perf_P4_Blue_InSeason_2x – most community footballers

Perf_P4_Black_InSeason_2x – higher level athletes in-season

D. Fallback / special weeks (non-negotiable)

You need at least:

Fallback_BusyWeek_2x – shorter, high-yield sessions

Fallback_Deload_2x – low volume/intensity

Fallback_HomeOnly_3x – bands / DBs

Those save you when life hits and you still want something in the app.
